Good To Know

Questions, answered.

What time does the party start?

+

Doors open at 22:00 and the lineup runs late into the night — get there early to avoid the queue.

Is there an age limit?

+

This is an 18+ event. Bring valid photo ID — you may be asked to show it at the door.

Is there a dress code?

+

Dress to impress — smart casual or streetwear works. Management reserves the right of admission.

How do I book a VIP table?

+

Message the reservations line directly on WhatsApp at 378 416 7006 and the team will confirm your table.

Are tickets refundable?

+

Tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to a friend — just message us their name ahead of the event.
